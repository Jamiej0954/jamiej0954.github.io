SHABR Project

The purpose of this project is to show the students' understanding of bootstrap through LOYO (learn on your own), where students are ask to learn a content by themselves and apply it in the project to show that they have fully master the skill.

Something that I learned from doing this project is that it's hard to learn a set of content on your own and it's helpful to ask you classmates for help. I wanted to thank my classmate, Elton, who helped me break down the component of carsouel and understand how it work.
